  • SEPTEMBER 2019

    INDUSTRY DAY

    Industry Day aims to harness and promote the power of Industry-Academia collaboration, kindle ideas for cogent partnerships and showcase cutting edge technology research conducted by the IIT Delhi research community with an objective to demonstrate technical research capabilities, forge new collaborative relationships and further strengthen our existing partnerships.

  • OCTOBER 2019

    DL 1-2-3-4 PROJECT EVALUATION

    With the onset of a new season, it is the time for all project evaluation. On this day, all technical and other projects going on in IIT are reviewed. The projects showcase the advancement in technology with innovation students and research communities bring into. With presentations and discussions, this day is very important for all the students and faculty members.

  • OCTOBER 2019

    RENDEZVOUS

    Rendezvous, famously known as RDV is the annual Cultural Festival of the Indian Institute of Technology, Delhi. It is a four-day-long event held in October every year. Being the second largest College Cultural Festival in Asia, it witnesses a footfall of more than 50k.

  • DECEMBER 2019

    ALUMNI DAY

    On Alumni Day, nominations for showcasing startup/innovation projects are selected. This is a flagship event of IITD and IITDAA held at the Campus and at Chapters. It is a day full of activities with focused Thematic Sessions, Entertainment and Cultural Music Program. With many industrialists and entrepreneurs, this is an event where we showcase the progress of the club.

  • FEBRUARY 2020

    AUTO EXPO

    The Auto Expo is a biennial automotive show held in Greater Noida, NCR, India. It is Asia's largest and the world's second-largest motor show. Auto Expo is one of the world’s premier auto shows. A signature event, that witnesses the best minds coming together on a single platform to showcases all that is best in the automotive world, in terms of products, technologies, concepts and trends.

  • MARCH 2020

    TRYST

    Tryst is the annual technical festival of the Indian Institute of Technology Delhi. The festival is the largest technical fest of North India with a huge footfall of more than 45,000 people around the globe. It is one of the largest and a highly acknowledged technical festival of India. The festival has always been driven by the motivation to bring together the world’s leading scientific minds along with renowned technologists and influential thinkers and connect them to a broader public involving students and faculty across India

  • APRIL 2020

    OPEN HOUSE

    Open House is a one-day event dedicated to exhibit an extensive collection of innovative research and product development projects. The day gives an insight into path-breaking research work, student projects and the numerous advanced facilities and laboratories available in IIT Delhi. Open House promises to be a real treat to the eyes of the connoisseurs by presenting finest of the projects to technology enthusiasts, students and colleagues from other technical Institutes, school children, industries and general public.
